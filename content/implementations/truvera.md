The **Truvera API** facilitates the issuance, verification, and revocation of VCs, along with DID management. **Truvera Workspace** offers a user-friendly platform for creating and managing DIDs, issuing VCs, and overseeing credential lifecycles. The **Credential Wallet SDK & White-Label Solutions** enable integration of credential management into existing applications or the deployment of a customizable wallet for securely storing and sharing VCs. **Credential Schemas** define the structure and attributes of VCs, ensuring consistency and compliance. The **Trust Registry** maintains an onchain list of authorized issuers and verifiers, ensuring that credentials are issued and validated by legitimate entities."
